Dossier

About us.

Fitness Cyborg is a modern fitness movement focused on inspiring active lifestyles through high-energy aerobics, group workouts, outdoor fitness activities, and live fitness events.

Founded by Makungo Muyembe, the brand was created to encourage people of all ages to embrace movement, discipline, confidence, and positive energy through engaging fitness experiences.

Fitness Cyborg combines fitness, music, motivation, and community interaction to create exciting workout environments that promote wellness and personal growth.

The mission is to build a strong fitness culture that motivates communities, students, organizations, and individuals to stay active and connected through movement.

Our Mission

To inspire healthier and more active communities through energetic fitness experiences, motivation, discipline, and positive movement culture.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading community fitness brands promoting wellness, movement, and active living through innovative fitness experiences and events.
